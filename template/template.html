Introduction

You are part of a team that has been sent to a recently discovered planet outside of the solar system. Your team will replace another team of fellow astronaut-scientists.

The other team has been exploring different objects and learning about the noises that these objects make. You are going to pick up where they left off and continue this exploration. As the first member of your team to wake up from hibernation, you must pass on any information you learn to the rest of your team members.

You'll learn about and explore 6 different kinds of objects in total. After each one, you will be asked short questions about them.

When you arrive on the planet, your laboratory will look like this:

You will have a box of objects. You can take an object out of the box to explore using the blue Pick Up button on the front of the box. It will be automatically moved to the blue stage. Then, you can drag the object to the green Testing Stage. You can press the green Test button to test whether or not an object produces a sound. After the object has been tested, it will be automatically moved to the pink Tested Items stage.

This is the end of the instructions. Hit the Continue button when you are ready to proceed to the laboratory.
