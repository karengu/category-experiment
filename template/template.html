Introduction

You are going to help teach children about a new kind of object.

First, your fellow teacher will teach you about the objects. Then, you will have time to explore them so that you can teach children about the objects later.

After you meet the teacher, you will enter the classroom. It will look like this:

You will have a box of objects. You can take an object out of the box to explore using the blue Pick Up button on the front of the box. It will be automatically moved to the blue stage. Then, you can drag the object to the green Testing Stage. You can press the green Test button to test whether or not an object produces a sound. After the object has been tested, it will be automatically moved to the pink Tested Items stage.

This is the end of the instructions. Hit the Continue button when you are ready to proceed to the classroom.
